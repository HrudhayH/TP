# **App Name**: Vriksa Global

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Hero Section: Displays a full-width, cinematic rice field visual at sunrise with a prominent headline and smooth parallax effects.
- Dynamic Product Categories Display: Presents product categories (Rice, Millets, Pulses, Spices, Dry Fruits) through elegant, interactive cards with premium photography placeholders.
- Featured Product Showcase: Highlights a specific product, such as rice, with beautiful macro photography, detailed text on quality, aroma, and export standards, utilizing elegant scrolling reveals.
- Global Presence Map & Logistics: Visually indicates Vriksa's global export presence, showcasing countries served, complemented by text on logistics capabilities.
- Vriksa Advantage Section: Clearly outlines Vriksa's core values: Quality assurance, sustainable sourcing, and global logistics, presented in a minimalist, spaced layout.
- AI-Enhanced Content Generation Tool: A backend tool to suggest and refine descriptive text for product attributes (quality, aroma) and 'Why choose Vriksa' sections, optimizing for clarity and regional market nuances.
- Interactive Call-to-Action: Prominently displays calls-to-action for users to explore products or contact the company, designed for clarity and engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A deep, muted olive-green (#3F4336) for text and core elements, conveying natural premium quality and grounded elegance, selected to evoke a sense of rich earth and natural agriculture.
- Background color: A very light, almost off-white with a subtle olive tint (#F7F7F6), providing an expansive canvas that highlights content and enhances the minimalist aesthetic.
- Accent color: A sophisticated, desaturated golden-ochre (#BF9633) used for subtle highlights, interactive elements, and key calls to action, inspired by cinematic sunrise glows and symbolizing premium quality.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for its artistic personality and clean, elegant lines, aligning with a premium, fashion-forward aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for its contemporary elegance and intellectual feel, ensuring readability for longer descriptive texts while maintaining a sophisticated tone.
- Use a set of minimal, clean line-art icons. Icons should be understated to maintain the elegant and minimalist UI, focusing on clarity over complexity. Custom-designed agricultural-themed icons where appropriate.
- Adopt an 'Apple-style' minimal layout featuring generous whitespace, precise grid alignments, and large, clear typography to ensure optimal readability and an uncrowded visual experience. Responsive design is paramount for seamless viewing across all devices.
- Implement smooth scrolling and subtle parallax effects for the hero and key content sections to create a cinematic and engaging user journey. Element reveals should use elegant, slow fade-in or subtle slide-up animations to maintain a premium feel without distraction.
